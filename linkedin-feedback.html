💬 How to Reply to Feedback (Very Important!)

  1. Reply fast: Within 30 min = maximum engagement
  2. Thank them by name: "Thank you, [Name]! Great point about..."
  3. Ask a follow-up: "Would you also highlight the RLS part when explaining this?"
  4. Save the good ones: Screenshot best explanations to learn from
  5. Follow the person: Build your developer network
  6. Take notes: Learn different angles for future interviews

📝 Sample Reply to a Comment

Thank you so much, [Name]! 🙌 Your framing of "solving multi-tenant security at the database level" is exactly the hook I was missing. I was trying to explain RLS technically, but your framing makes it feel like a real business decision. Would you also emphasize the "no framework" choice as a deliberate learning decision? Or would you skip that detail? Really appreciate you taking the time — this teaches me more than any tutorial. Following you to keep learning 🚀

🎯 Why This Strategy Works

  • Recruiters notice: Humble learner = good hire

  • Network growth: Every commenter = potential connection
  • Algorithm boost: LinkedIn rewards posts with many comments
  • Job leads: Someone might say "we're hiring, apply!"
  • Real learning: You'll internalize how to explain projects
